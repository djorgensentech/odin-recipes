Yaroa

Description

Yaroa is a traditional Dominican dish that is full of yummy ingredients like cheese, meat, and awesome seasoning and sauce.

I recently made my roomates Yaroa and they loved it! You can try various meats like beef and chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup Tomato Sauce
  • 2 tsp Garlic
  • 1 lb Beef
  • Optional: 1/2 cup Onions

Steps

  1. Make the Fries
  2. Make the Beef Mixture
    1. Thaw the meat
    2. Add sauce
    3. Cook the meat
  3. Layer the beef over fries
  4. Cover with ketchup and mayo
