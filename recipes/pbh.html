Peanut Butter and Honey Sandwich

Description

A Peanut Butter and Honey sandwich is a delicious food enjoyed by the famous Disney character Winnie the Pooh. It is a delicious blend of sweet and savory.

This recipe can be made with creamy or chunky peanut butter. If using chunky peanut butter, add more honey to balance out the sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Slices Bread
  • 3 Tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 4 Tbsp Honey

Steps

  1. Add peanut butter to one slice of bread
  2. To add the honey, either
    • Add to the other slice
    • Add to both slices
  3. Combine the two slices of bread
