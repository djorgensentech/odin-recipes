Coquito

Description

Coquito is a creamy, tropical, Puerto Rican holiday drink with tons of coconut flavor. It's made with both evaporated and condensed milk, cream of coconut, spices, rum, and — in this version — egg yolks. It is always requested at my holiday gatherings. Sometimes I hide it in the fridge to keep for myself! Serve in glass cups and sprinkle with more cinnamon if desired. Feliz Navidad! (Brandy, allrecipes.com)

This is a recipe that I want to try out this year for Christmas. I will make it non-alcoholic, so those ingredients have been removed.

Ingredients

  • 2 Beaten Egg Yolks
  • 1 Can Cream of Cocount
  • 1 Can Sweetened condensed milk
  • To Taste Water
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • To Taste Cinnamon and Cloves

Steps

  1. Cook the evaporated milk and egg yolks in a double boiler until thick.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend with the remaining ingredients.
  3. Transfer to glass bottles and refrigerator until cold.
